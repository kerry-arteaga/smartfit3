Meet Your Trainer

Hello! My name is Moses Arteaga and I have been a personal trainer for almost a decade now. Over the past 10 years, I have been sharpening my skills along with my clients.

I competed in wrestling in high school through college which instilled the importance of physical fitness and motivation in me.

My goal is to have the most positive influence that I can in the lives of the people that I train, helping them become the healthiest and happiest version of themselves.

The more you know, the more you grow!
